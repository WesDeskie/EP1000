Welcome to my website!

This website was created to document what I have learnt during my Digital Fabrication & Prototyping Fundamentals module. I go through topics I was taught by compiling what I've learnt into short simple tutorials that hopefully allow you to understand the processes and possibly recreate the various learning projects.

I also document the process of designing, fabricating, testing and troubleshooting of my Final Project, which puts all the skills and knowledge I have attained to the test. If you want to find out more about me and how to contact me, you can click here. Enjoy!
