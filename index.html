Digital Fabrication Website

Welcome to my website!

This website was created to document what I have learnt during my Digital Fabrication & Prototyping Fundamentals module. I go through topics I was taught such as web development, Computer Aided Design, and Fabrication by compiling what I've learnt into short simple tutorials that hopefully allow you to understand the processes and possibly recreate the various learning projects. I also document the process of designing, fabricating, testing and troubleshooting of my Final Project, which puts all the skills and knowledge I have attained to the test. You can find out more about me by clicking here. Or if you have any questions or queries about my projects/tutorials, you can visit my Contact Page here. Enjoy!
